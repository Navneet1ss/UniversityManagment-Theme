Our Mission

As there is a rise in health issues, providing health assistance to each and every member is important. People nowadays are busy with their lives and even they forget about their health problems. Some people do not even take care of their health. Our mission is to see a healthier world by delivering our medical services to the people.

Our Values

We will fulfill our mission by extending The New School's legacy as a nontraditional university and community, nimble and responsive to change, that:

Extend knowledge

To advance and extend knowledge, learning, and culture, especially throughout California.

Professionally Devlopment

To provide opportunities for individuals to develop intellectually, personally, and professionally.

Educational Approach

Places collaborative, project-based learning at the center of the educational experience

Multi-Cultural Society

To prepare students for international, multi-cultural society and provide public services that enrich the university and its communities.

Humanity And Culture

Prioritizes humanity and culture in designing systems and environments to improve the human condition, an approach that draws on design thinking and the liberal & performing arts

Excellent Education

To encourage and provide access to an excellent education to all who are prepared for and wish to participate in collegiate study.
